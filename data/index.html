      Advanced

      Timing

      Pause at DUMP

      Seconds the motor stays STOPPED at the DUMP sensor so waste can fall through the dump door. The motor does not rotate past DUMP — on real Litter Robot mechanics, continuing past DUMP would close the door again. For Empty, this is how long to wait there before returning home (e.g. enough time to pull the tray).

      Level overshoot (sand shake)

      After the CW return reaches HOME, the motor keeps going CW for this many seconds (first half of the shake), reverses CCW back through HOME and continues another 5 s past HOME (hardcoded back-shake), then reverses CW one last time and stops at HOME. The double oscillation distributes the sand evenly. Set to 0 to disable both halves and stop directly at HOME.
